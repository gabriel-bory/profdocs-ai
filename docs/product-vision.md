# Product Vision

## Product Name

**ProfDocs AI — Professional Document Intelligence Platform**

## Problem

Professionals often work with scattered documents, notes, reports and technical files. Finding relevant information inside those documents can be slow, repetitive and error-prone.

## Vision

ProfDocs AI aims to become a professional document intelligence platform that helps users transform documents into searchable knowledge bases and ask questions grounded in source content.

## Initial Scope

The project will start with a small, realistic MVP:

- Full-stack foundation
- Document ingestion for TXT and Markdown
- Chunking
- Semantic search
- Minimal RAG
- Source-based answers

## Target Users

The initial target users are professionals who work with structured or semi-structured documents, including developers, researchers, analysts, consultants and technical teams.

## Long-Term Vision

Future versions may include PDF support, DOCX support, OCR, webhooks, external integrations, report generation, AI agents and local model support.

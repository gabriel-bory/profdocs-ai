---

**ProfDocs AI** is a full-stack AI document intelligence platform.

The long-term goal is to help professionals transform documents into searchable knowledge bases and ask questions grounded in source content.

## Initial Scope

The project starts small and evolves in phases:

- Full-stack foundation
- TXT/Markdown document ingestion
- Chunking
- Semantic search
- Minimal RAG
- Source-based answers

## Long-Term Vision

Future versions may include:

- PDF support
- DOCX support
- OCR
- Webhooks
- External integrations
- Report generation
- AI agents
- Local model support
